About Us

About karnataka art relief Fund

We are living in unprecedented, uncertain times. The world has been shocked by the devastating effects of COVID-19. We are aware of the burden that COVID-19 is having on the music community and we are actively taking steps to provide assistance where it’s needed most. Due to massive cancellations, artists are struggling to find the means to make ends meet during these unprecedented times. Artists are struggling financially due to the COVID-19 pandemic. Community of musicians came together to create a fund and provide support to the artist community. Contributions to the General Musicians Fund will help to pay for an artist’s medical expenses, housing, food and other vital expenses for livelihood. .

Why Relief for artists ?

As artists, culture bearers, creative workers, freelancers, and more lose work and income due to cancellations and closings related to the Coronavirus/COVID-19 pandemic, we look to share resources to support our community. Karnataka Corona Art Relief Fund is a resource dedicated to supporting artists in Karnataka who experience career-threatening emergencies. .

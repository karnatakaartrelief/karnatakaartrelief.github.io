Why Relief for artists ?

As artists, culture bearers, creative workers, freelancers, and more lose work and income due to cancellations and closings related to the Coronavirus/COVID-19 pandemic, we look to share resources to support our community. Due to massive cancellations, artists are struggling to find the means to make ends meet during these unprecedented times. Artists are struggling financially due to the COVID-19 pandemic. Karnataka Corona Art Relief Fund is a resource dedicated to supporting artists in Karnataka who experience emergencies. .
